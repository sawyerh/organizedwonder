Marilyn Myller

Marilyn maketh, Marilyn taketh awayth. Directed by MIKEY PLEASE A year in the making, the full six minute stopmotion short features the voice of Josie Long, one zillion hand carved tiny things, literally tens of carved foam puppets, two eye fulls of in-camera, long-exposure light trickery and a pair of tiny dolphins, smooching.
